Useful prompt phrases

Copy a prompt into Gemini, Perplexity, or your favourite AI tool, then replace the placeholders like [topic].

Pareto Principle Learning Plan

I want to learn [topic]. Can you use the Pareto Principle, which identifies the 20% of the topic that will yield 80% of the desired results, to create a focused learning plan for me?

Learning Resources Suggestion

Suggest various learning resources (books, videos, podcasts, interactive exercises) for [topic] that cater to different learning styles.

Explain in Simple Terms

Explain [topic] to me in the simplest terms possible as if I were a complete beginner.

Mental Models & Analogies

Create mental models or analogies to help me understand and remember [topics].

Visualization Exercise

Guide me through a visualization exercise to help me internalize [topic] and imagine myself successfully applying it to a real-life situation.

Socratic Method

I want you to act as Socrates and use the Socratic Method to help me improve my critical thinking, logic, and reasoning skills. Ask me open-ended questions about what I say; after each answer, give constructive feedback before asking the next question.

Socratic Tutor

Act as a Socratic tutor and help me understand [topic]. Ask questions to guide my understanding and give feedback after each reply before moving on.

Semantic Propositions Table

Make a list of propositions for this [vocab list] in the formats “X is a type of Y”, “W is caused by X”, and “A explains B”. Put them in a table with four columns: term, proposition, type (Classification / Cause–Effect / Explanation), and explanation.

Feynman Technique

Break down [topic] into core components using the Feynman Technique, then quiz me step by step until I can explain it simply in my own words.

Role-Playing Scenario

Teach me [topic] through a role-playing scenario where I practise applying ideas in a simulated real-world situation, with feedback after each step.

Compare & Contrast

Compare and contrast [topic] with [related topic] in a table: similarities, differences, and when to use each.

Mind Map Design

Design a text-based mind map for [topic], starting from the central idea and branching into subtopics, examples, and connections.

Story / Narrative Learning

Turn [topic] into a memorable story or narrative. Weave key ideas into the plot so I can remember them through the storyline.

Debate Both Sides

Debate both sides of a controversial angle on [topic] with me: argue for and against, then help me refine my stance with counterpoints.

Teach-Back Challenge

After I have studied [topic], ask me to teach it back to you as if you were a beginner. Comment on clarity, accuracy, and depth, then suggest how I can explain it better.
